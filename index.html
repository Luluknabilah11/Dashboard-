🌏 Pacific Region Climate Change Dashboard

An interactive dashboard designed to explore the relationship between carbon emissions, temperature anomalies, disaster impacts, and economic losses across countries in the Pacific region.

Background

Climate change has become one of the most significant global challenges, affecting ecosystems, human well-being, and economic sustainability worldwide. Pacific countries are particularly vulnerable due to their geographical characteristics, including extensive coastlines and small island territories.

This dashboard presents an interactive data storytelling approach to analyze the relationship between greenhouse gas emissions, temperature anomalies, disaster impacts, and direct economic losses across Pacific countries.
